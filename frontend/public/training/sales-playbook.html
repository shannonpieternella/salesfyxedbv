Official 4-Step Customer Vibe Script

📞 THE PROVEN CONVERSION SCRIPT

STEP 1: CUSTOMER VIBE (Zero Sales)

"Hi, quick practical question: How fast do you usually respond when someone calls or makes an inquiry?"

→ Start as potential customer, not salesperson

→ Focus on their service speed/quality

→ 85% engage because it sounds like customer research

FIRST YES = Problem Recognition

STEP 2: AVAILABILITY CHECK

"Great, clear. And are you actually taking new customers now, or are you completely booked?"

→ Confirms they want new business

→ Sounds like you're considering them

→ Gets them in "customer acquisition" mindset

SECOND YES = Business Opportunity Confirmed

STEP 3: SMOOTH TRANSITION

"Perfect, good to hear there's room for new customers. I'm [name] from Fyxed B.V. in Arnhem. We help businesses in the region get 10+ extra appointments per month with an AI Receptionist that never misses a call."

→ Transparent reveal builds trust

→ Immediate value proposition

→ Localized and specific benefit

As a bonus, we also reactivate your old leads that are gathering dust. Shall I install a free AI Receptionist for you so you can see how many extra appointments come in?"

Advanced Implementation

🎯 MASTERING THE CUSTOMER VIBE APPROACH

Voice tone: Curious and helpful, never salesy

Pacing: Natural conversation speed, not rushed

Questions: Genuine interest in their business

Transition: Smooth reveal builds trust, not deception

❓ "How do I handle rejection on the customer vibe opening?"

If they say "We're not interested in sales calls":

"Oh no, I'm not selling anything - I'm actually considering your services and wanted to know about your response time. Are you taking new customers?"

Result: 90% switch back to helpful mode and answer your question.

🤖 "What if they ask technical questions about the AI?"

Keep it simple: "It's like having a 24/7 employee who never gets tired, never misses calls, and works for pennies per conversation. The technical stuff happens behind the scenes - you just see more appointments."

Focus on outcomes, not features. Business owners care about results, not technology.

💰 "How do I justify the cost if they think it's expensive?"

Reframe with ROI: "One new customer typically covers 2-3 months of AI costs. Our clients usually see 10+ extra appointments per month. Even if only half convert, you're still ahead."

Use comparison: "It's like hiring a part-time employee for €400/month who works 24/7 and never calls in sick."

⏰ "When do I see results from the AI installation?"

Set expectations: "Usually within 1-2 weeks you'll see the AI capturing leads and booking appointments. For lead reactivation, results start showing within 2-4 weeks as the AI processes your existing database."

Follow-up schedule: Weekly check-ins for the first month to optimize and show progress.

🏆 "How do I ask for referrals after successful implementation?"

After proven results: "I see you've had 15 extra appointments this month from the AI. Do you know any other business owners who might be losing opportunities like you were? I'd love to help them too."

Incentive offer: "For every referral that installs our AI, we'll give you a month of free usage credits."
